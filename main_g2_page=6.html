DNA Guide

S2A Interactive built the landmark website for DNA Guide AB, the first company to offer personal home DNA testing for individuals in Sweden, and also the first in Europe!

This unique opportunity presented a number of challenges, beginning with privacy and security issues, which were solved by creating a customized SMS one time password verification system and a secure back-office with multiple points of authentication, as well as more common usability and information provisional tasks which we have great depth of experience. We are proud to help DNA Guide carry Europe into the future of genetics and information sciences.

(Currently only available in Swedish)
