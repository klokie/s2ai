SURFACE TO AIR was officially founded in 2000 as a means by which to explore the roles of art and technology in emerging commercial communications, and to produce revolutionary and thought-provoking works of brilliance. Beginning with its network hub in downtown New York as the official base of operations and extending its reach into Europe via the design and production studio in Paris, SURFACE TO AIR now runs its online and interactive operations from Stockholm, Sweden.
